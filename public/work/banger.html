Banger Gallery

Website

As a designer with an interest in the niche of rap mixtape artwork, Banger Gallery was created to explore the correlation between artist locality and styles of artwork. I took the top 500 mixtapes on DatPiff, and wrote a program to determine the predominant color palette of the cover and calculate the visual complexity based on those results. This was done as my final project for the Web Development Immersive at General Assembly.
